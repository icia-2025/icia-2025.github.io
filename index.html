About


Sponsored by the IEEE Robotics and Automation society, the 17th event of the ICIA conference series, the 2025 IEEE International Conference on Information Automation (IEEE ICIA 2025) will be held in Nyingchi, Tibet, China, July 28-30, 2025, which offers a medium for researchers and practitioners to exchange and explore the issues and opportunities of electrical and computer engineering research and development from China and around the world. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements. The IEEE ICIA 2025 conference promises to offer the participants a great experience with excellent technical and social programs. High quality papers reporting original research results and innovations in all aspects of information, automation, artificial intelligence, robotics, image processing, computer vision, DSP, BME and related areas as well as their applications are invited.

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society is committed to advancing diversity in Robotics and Automation, and to promoting an inclusive and equitable culture that welcomes, engages, and rewards all who contribute to the field, without regard to ethnicity, religion, gender, disability, age, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, or gender expression.



Important Dates

Submission of full papers in PDF and organized session proposals: May 1, 2025
Notification of paper acceptance: June 1, 2025
Submission of final camera-ready papers: June 15, 2025
